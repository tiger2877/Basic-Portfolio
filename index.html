About Me

My name is Budhi Cahyadi. I am currently enrolled in the University of Toronto SCS Coding Boot Camps because I have a passion for learning and staying on top of the latest in web development. And, I enjoy solving problems and making the seemingly impossible possible.

Web development has always been my passion ever since I developed my first offical website for Towson University International Students Association back in 2004.

As for my day job, I am a Systems Administrator for OCR Canada, a subsidiary of Barcodes, Inc. I have more than 3 years of NetSuite experience as a Certified NetSuite Administrator and Git proficiency. My happy place is when I can design something that solves the need of our NetSuite users to have a system that is fully functional.
